# Udyam Registration — Step-by-Step Guide

## What is Udyam?
Udyam is the Indian government's free MSME (Micro, Small & Medium Enterprise) registration. It gives you an official business identity — accepted by Meta for Business Verification.

---

## Prerequisites

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Aadhaar Card** | Linked to mobile number (OTP verification required) |
| **PAN Card** | Personal PAN (no need for business PAN) |
| **Mobile Number** | Linked to Aadhaar for OTP |
| **Email ID** | For receiving the certificate |
| **Bank Account** | Account number + IFSC code |
| **Business Details** | Name, type, start date, address |

---

## Step-by-Step Registration

### Step 1: Go to the Portal
- Open: [https://udyamregistration.gov.in](https://udyamregistration.gov.in)
- Click **"For New Entrepreneurs who are not Registered yet as MSME"**

### Step 2: Enter Aadhaar Details
- Enter your **Aadhaar Number**
- Enter your **Name** (as per Aadhaar)
- Click **"Validate & Generate OTP"**
- Enter the OTP received on your Aadhaar-linked mobile

### Step 3: PAN Verification
- Select PAN type: **Personal**
- Enter your **PAN Number**
- Click **"Validate PAN"**
- Your name and details will auto-fill from PAN records

### Step 4: Fill Enterprise Details

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Enterprise Name** | `DM Shiyam` (or your preferred business name) |
| **Type of Enterprise** | `Proprietorship` |
| **Type of Organisation** | `Services` |
| **Date of Commencement** | Today's date or when you started the project |
| **Gender** | Your gender |
| **Category** | Your category (General / OBC / SC / ST) |
| **Physically Handicapped** | Yes / No |

### Step 5: Plant / Unit Details

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Unit Name** | `DM Shiyam` |
| **Address** | Your home address |
| **State / District** | Your state and district |
| **Pin Code** | Your area pin code |
| **Mobile** | Your mobile number |
| **Email** | Your email address |

### Step 6: Bank Details

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Bank Account Number** | Your savings/current account number |
| **IFSC Code** | Your bank's IFSC code |

### Step 7: Major Activity & NIC Code

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Major Activity** | Select **"Services"** |
| **NIC 2-digit Code** | `62` — Computer programming, consultancy and related activities |
| **NIC 5-digit Code** | `62011` — Computer programming activities (custom software development) |
| **Activity Description** | `Software development - Instagram automation SaaS platform` |

### Step 8: Investment & Turnover

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **Investment in Plant & Machinery** | `0` or actual amount (if any) |
| **Turnover** | `0` (if no revenue yet) |

> With ₹0 investment and ₹0 turnover, you'll be classified as a **Micro Enterprise** — perfectly fine for Meta verification.

### Step 9: Submit

> ✅ **Registration Complete!**
- Check the **declaration checkbox**
- Click **"Submit & Get Final OTP"**
- Enter the final OTP
- Click **"Submit"**

### Step 10: Download Certificate

- After submission, your **Udyam Registration Certificate** will be generated
- It will show your **Udyam Registration Number** (e.g., `UDYAM-MH-00-0000000`)
- **Download the PDF** — you'll upload this to Meta
- A copy will also be sent to your email

---

## Sample Certificate Info

Your certificate will contain:
- Udyam Registration Number
- Enterprise Name (DM Shiyam)
- Type: Micro Enterprise
- Major Activity: Services
- NIC Code: 62011
- Owner Name, Address, PAN, Aadhaar (masked)

---

## After Registration — Use for Meta

1. Go to **Meta Business Suite** → **Settings** → **Business Info**
2. Click **"Start Verification"**
3. Enter:
   - **Legal Business Name**: Same as on Udyam certificate
   - **Address**: Same as on Udyam certificate
   - **Phone**: Your registered mobile number
4. Upload the **Udyam Registration Certificate PDF**
5. Submit and wait 1-3 business days for approval

## FAQ

**Q: Is this free?**
A: Yes, 100% free. No charges at any step.

**Q: Can I do this as a salaried employee?**
A: Yes. No conflict with employment. Thousands of employees have Udyam registrations for side projects.

**Q: Do I need to pay any tax?**
A: Not until you earn revenue. Even then, it's just regular ITR filing.

**Q: Can I cancel it later?**
A: Yes, you can deactivate/cancel your Udyam registration anytime.

**Q: How long does it take?**
A: 10-15 minutes. Certificate is generated instantly after final OTP.

**Q: What if my Aadhaar mobile number is different?**
A: You must use the mobile number linked to your Aadhaar. Update it at your nearest Aadhaar center if needed.

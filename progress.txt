## Critical decision made 2026-07-12
SWITCH from old Facebook Login for Business flow to NEW Instagram Business Login flow.

### Why we switched
- Current submission had 6 permissions (instagram_basic, instagram_manage_comments,
  instagram_manage_messages, pages_show_list, pages_manage_metadata, pages_read_engagement)
- pages_read_engagement had no legitimate justification → guaranteed rejection risk
- New flow needs only 3 permissions, all clearly justified
- No FB Page dependency for end users
